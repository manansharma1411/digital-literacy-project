## Responsible Social Media Use Checklist

### **Do’s**

* **Think before posting** — ensure your content is appropriate and respectful.
* **Maintain privacy settings** — control who can see your posts and personal information.
* **Verify information before sharing** — avoid spreading misinformation or fake news.
* **Use social media for learning and networking** — follow educational pages and professionals.
* **Be respectful in interactions** — communicate politely and avoid online conflicts.

### **Don’ts**

* **Do not share sensitive personal information** (address, phone number, OTPs, passwords).
* **Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content** that may harm your reputation.
* **Do not engage in cyberbullying or trolling** — it can have serious consequences.
* **Avoid oversharing your daily activities or location** for safety reasons.
* **Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages** — they may be scams or phishing attempts.
