### Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz

As part of this project, a **Google Form** has been created to assess and improve digital literacy awareness among batchmates.
The quiz consists of **5 questions**, including:

* Multiple choice questions
* Short answer questions

It is designed to evaluate basic understanding of safe internet practices, digital tools, and responsible online behavior.

**Access the Quiz Here:**
(https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfV1klLsruFJp7Av8GJQVy3UCmtqP82whYUIJ3UvmUfF2ua2g/viewform?usp=dialog)


### Response Collection & Analysis

All responses submitted through the form are automatically recorded and organized using **Google Sheets**, enabling easy analysis and interpretation of the data.
