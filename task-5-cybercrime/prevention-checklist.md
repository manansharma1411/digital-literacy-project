## **Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist**

1. **Never share OTPs, passwords, or UPI PINs** with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank or a trusted service.
2. **Always verify links before clicking**—avoid suspicious URLs received via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
3. **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)** on all important accounts (email, social media, banking apps).
4. **Download apps only from official sources** like Google Play Store or Apple App Store—avoid APK files from unknown links.
5. **Keep your devices updated** with the latest security patches and antivirus protection.
6. **Be cautious on public Wi-Fi**—avoid logging into banking or personal accounts on unsecured networks.

### **UPI & Financial Safety Tips**

7. **Remember: entering your UPI PIN means you are paying money, not receiving it.** Never approve unknown “Collect Requests.”
8. **Double-check the receiver’s name before sending money**—even small mistakes can lead to loss.
9. **Avoid sharing screenshots of transactions** that contain sensitive details like UPI IDs or QR codes.

### **Social & Personal Safety**

10. **Adjust privacy settings on social media**—limit who can view your personal information.
11. **Do not engage with unknown callers or messages asking for urgent financial action.** Take time to verify.

### **Reporting Cybercrime**

12. **Immediately report suspicious activity or fraud:**

* National Cyber Crime Portal: [https://cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in)
* Helpline Number: 1930
